Nova Labs is a start up working on the development of multiple Blockchain and AI projects. Our main project is
LennyVerse. It can be defined as a Decentralized Art Ecosystem and Story-Oriented Character Token that 
is deployed on Binance SmartChain (BSC). Our vision is to build a strong and reliable dapp 
ecosystem to promote content creation and digital art development, while carrying messages and 
supporting humanitarian causes. Our primary mission is to provide the community with the fairest and most 
reliable project, while fostering innovation and progress.
